## Overview

This category explains how to organize your flags across different environments in your LaunchDarkly project. From the flag dashboard, you can see all the environments a flag exists in, and make changes that you can apply across multiple environments.

In addition, this category has content about the major organizational structures LaunchDarkly uses: projects and environments.
