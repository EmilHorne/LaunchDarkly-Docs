## Overview

This topic explains how to manage teams and team members in LaunchDarkly and shows you how to use the Teams tab.

## Adding a member to an existing team

You can add members to a team when you create a team, or you can add members to existing teams. To learn more about creating teams, read [Creating a team](/home/teams/creating).

If you have an existing team and you want to add a member to it:

1. Navigate to [Account settings](https://app.launchdarkly.com/settings).
2. Click into the [Teams](https://app.launchdarkly.com/settings/teams) tab.
3. Click **Manage**. The "Manage" screen appears.
4. Click into the "Members" list and type the name of the member you wish to add to the team.
5. Select the member to add to the team.
6. Click the **Save team** button.

## Viewing an individual member's teams

Members of your organization can belong to multiple teams.

To view the teams that a member is on:

1. Navigate to [Members](https://app.launchdarkly.com/settings/members).
2. Filter the list of members for the member you're looking for.
3. Find the "Teams" column. The teams of which that member is a part are listed here. There may be additional teams in the overflow menu.

## Removing a member from a team

You can remove a member from a team in two different ways. You can use the Team management page or the Members page.

To remove a member from the team management page:

1. Navigate to the [Account settings](https://app.launchdarkly.com/settings) page.
2. Click into the [Teams](https://app.launchdarkly.com/settings/teams) tab and find the team that you want to remove the member from.
3. Click **Manage**.
4. Find the member in the list of team members.
5. Click the **X** next to their name to remove them from the team.
6. Click **Save Team**.

To remove a member from the **Members** page:

1. Navigate to the [Members](https://app.launchdarkly.com/settings/members) page.
2. Find the member in the list of team members.
3. Click on the **Manage** button.
4. On the list of "Team membership and roles", find the team that you want to remove the member from.
5. Click **Remove Member**.
6. Click **Delete**.

## Deleting a team

LaunchDarkly account members with the `owner / admin` role can delete teams from the **Teams** tab.

To delete a team:

1. Log in to LaunchDarkly as an `owner / admin`.
2. Navigate to the [Account settings](https://app.launchdarkly.com/settings) page.
3. Click into the [Teams](https://app.launchdarkly.com/settings/teams) tab.
4. Find the team you wish to delete and click **Manage**.
5. Scroll to the bottom of the team management page and click **Delete Team.** A confirmation appears.
6. Click **Delete.**
